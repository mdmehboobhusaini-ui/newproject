Last Updated: April 2026

At Teen Minar (accessible at teenminar.com and teenminar.in), we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our website.

Teen Minar is a static website that does not require user registration or login. We do not collect any personal information such as names, email addresses, phone numbers, or payment details.

We take reasonable measures to protect the information collected through our website. Our website is served over HTTPS (SSL/TLS encryption) to ensure secure data transmission. Since we do not collect personal data, the risk of data breach is minimal.

Our website may contain links to external websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of external sites. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any external websites you visit.
